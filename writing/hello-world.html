Hello World

03/30/2022

I decided to make a blog, so I figured I’d introduce myself first—my name’s Andrew. I’m originally from Texas, but I currently live in NYC and attend New York University (where I play baseball and study business). I hope you get to learn more about me as you read my content.

Why I'm writing

I created this blog to have a place where I’d rather be original than right. I want to document and share some of my thoughts and experiences. For the past few years, I really haven’t been writing that much. I never thought I’d miss writing school essays or even be interested in keeping a journal, but I think there is so much value in creating things other people can see.

I’ve been going back and forth on creating this for a while now, and I had doubt for a few reasons:

  • I was worried that I’d never be writing my real opinions down if I knew someone was reading them, so my content wouldn’t be as genuine.
  • Between baseball, school, and work, I don’t have the time.
  • I was afraid that putting my thoughts into some centralized format wouldn’t leave me much of a story left to tell in person.

Recently, I was re-inspired by a couple of friends to create this. I think the key is it has to be something I enjoy, and I’ll only get better and figure things out by doing it.

Looking forward

I’m gonna shoot from the hip. The editing is going to be light, and you’ll probably find some errors. As far as topics I want to explore, I see myself talking about baseball, academia, finance, and programming, as well as just describing some of the experiences I have. But honestly, I don't really have a clear direction for where this is going yet. We'll see what happens.

Thank you for reading.
